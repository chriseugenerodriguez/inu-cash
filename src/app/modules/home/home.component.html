How to buy $INU

01

Create a MetaMask Wallet

$INU token is available on the Ethereum blockchain. MetaMask is the market leader in ERC20 (Ethereum) wallets. On Google Chrome, visit metamask.io to download the extension and set up a wallet. On mobile? Get MetaMask's app for iPhone or Android.

02

Send $ETH to MetaMask

Buy Ethereum through MetaMask or transfer it to your MetaMask wallet address from another wallet (e.g. Coinbase or Binance).

03

Visit INU Swap (coming soon)

You can currently buy $INU on INU Swap, INU's official decentralized exchange where you can swap any ERC20 token.

 04

Swap $ETH for $INU

Enter the amount of $ETH you would like to swap for $INU. Click Connect Wallet then Swap.
